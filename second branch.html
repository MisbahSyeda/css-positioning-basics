Number 1 in D-A-CH XING has been writing its own success story for 15 years. The Company was founded in Hamburg, Germany, in 2003, has been publicly listed since 2006, and listed on the TecDAX since September 2011. XING became a unicorn company in 2015. XING has over 1500 employees spread across offices in Hamburg, Munich, Barcelona, Vienna, Zurich and Porto.

What is XING?

XING is the social network for business professionals. Almost 15 million members are currently registered on XING (as of December 2018). It is a platform where professionals from all kinds of different industries can meet up, find jobs, colleagues, new assignments, cooperation partners, experts and generate business ideas. Members can meet and exchange views in over 80,000 specialist groups, while also getting together at networking events.
